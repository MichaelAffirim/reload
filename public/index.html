ABOUT||US

Accra Technical University Libraries is the library system of Accra Technical University, serving the university's students and faculty or departments. The Libraries collectively hold some 5 million volumes.The collection contains 3.5 million manuscripts, 1 million public documents, and tens of thousands of films and videos. The ATUL consists of the School of Applied Sciences Library located on the K-block,School of Applied Arts Library,Business School Library on West Campus. It also includes the Library Service Center, library offices located in the Business School library, as well as a few other departments.
